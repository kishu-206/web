About the chapter

Who we are

A student community at VIT Vellore that learns technology in a structured, practical way.

Mission

To create a friendly space for students to explore engineering and computer science topics through workshops, projects, and peer learning.

Vision

Encourage curious minds to build, research, and share knowledge, while connecting with the wider IEEE community.

Values

Simple principles that guide our events.

Learn

Start from basics, ask questions, and build understanding step-by-step.

Build

Turn ideas into small projects, then improve them using feedback.

Share

Present, write, and help others learn—knowledge grows in a community.
