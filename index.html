Technical • Community • Impact

Building engineers through hands-on technology, research, and collaboration.

IEEE VIT Vellore is a student-led chapter focused on learning by doing: workshops, projects, talks, and events that make technology approachable.

What we do

  • Workshops Beginner-friendly sessions on tools & fundamentals
  • Projects Build and ship real solutions with teams
  • Talks Industry & research discussions, Q&A included
  • Community Peer learning, mentorship, and volunteering

Featured focus areas

Why join?

  • Build confidence by presenting and sharing work.
  • Meet people who enjoy making things together.
  • Explore research, publications, and competitions.
